3D Maze Game

A thrilling adventure through a maze in 3D space!

Key Features

Randomly generated mazes

The game generates a new maze every time you play, so each game is a unique experience. The mazes are designed to be challenging but solvable, so you'll need to use your puzzle-solving skills to find the way out.

3D graphics and immersive sound

The game features stunning 3D graphics that make you feel like you're really exploring a maze in space. The sound effects and music add to the immersive experience, making you feel like you're really there.

Compete with others on the leaderboard

See how you stack up against other players on the leaderboard! The game tracks your score and time, and you can submit your best scores to see where you rank.

About the Project

The 3D Maze Game project was inspired by our love for classic arcade games and our passion for creating interactive experiences. As a team of Holberton School students, we were excited to take on this challenge and showcase our skills in game development.

The project took us several weeks to complete, from brainstorming ideas to coding, testing and debugging. It was a challenging but rewarding experience, and we are proud of the final result.

This project is part of our portfolio for Holberton School, where we are learning software engineering
